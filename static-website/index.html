Invitations are Out!

March 9th 2016


Invitations are now out there, and we have been getting back some RSVPs. which is exciting! But we have also been receiving some questions, so hopefully anyone coming to the site can find the answers.

Everyone who has an invitation addressed to a single person gets a plus one guest. Please write your guest's name on the RSVP- it will make it easier for us to include them in the final count and have a table place card for them :). If we get an RSVP back without any indication that you are bringing a guest, that's perfectly fine, but we'll assume you aren't bringing a guest if you haven't contacted us otherwise.
Yes, we would like you to mail out the RSVPs :). We will accomodate emails, texts, and phone calls to the best of our ability, but it makes it a lot easier for us if we get your RSVP card back- and it's fun getting them back in the mail as well!
Please indicate any special food allergies or dietary restrictions on the RSVP card you send back to us, so that we can make the reception venue aware in the final count.

If there are any other questions, please feel free to contact us directly. Hope to see you all in April!
