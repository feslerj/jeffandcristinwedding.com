The Venue


The Waterview Loft At The Port of Detroit

The reception starts at 6:00 PM, and Waterview Loft has requested that guests not arrive any earlier than that to the venue. Here are some of our favorite places that you may want to hang your hat while in Downtown Detroit waiting for our reception to start.



The Waterview Loft is located inside The Detroit Port Authority right upon The Detroit River Walk on Atwater Street in Downtown Detroit.

There is free, completementary valet parking at the venue. Guests staying at Greektown may want to park at Greektown and take The People Mover, to the Renaissance Center stop and walk to Waterview Loft. More details on this are located on the Hotel page.

Directions to the Reception


130 Atwater Street, Detroit, MI 48207

Those using Über for the first time can use this code to get $20 off their first ride using the service, compliments of the father of the groom: Dkm92ue. You can download Über to any Android or iPhone.

By car, take I-96 East, following signs for M-10 South until the freeway ends at Jefferson Avenue, after exiting underneath Cobo Center from M-10, The Lodge Freeway. After the first light, get all the way over to the right lane, and you will turn right on Bates Street. Bates St. will be the first right turn available after exiting the freeway on to Jefferson. Continue down to Atwater street where you will find the venue and parking.
